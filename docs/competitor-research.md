# Competitor research notes (differentiation only)

Reviewed patterns from Herman Miller/MillerKnoll, Steelcase, Haworth, Branch, Humanscale, IKEA Business, West Elm, Article, Burrow, Crate and Barrel, Room and Board, HON and National Office Furniture.

Common expectations:
- Category + space navigation
- Clear PDP dimensions and material selectors
- Comparison and planning tools
- Shipping/assembly guidance
- Mobile browsing with sticky commerce actions
- Office/residential crossover merchandising

Brightdesk differentiation:
- Organize primarily around five activity zones (Focus, Meet, Store, Welcome, Reset)
- Specification-first truthfulness (no unverified ergonomic/commercial claims)
- Footprint index + measuring-route emphasis
- Workspace builder without fake bundles
- Warm architectural visual system, not corporate blue or beige lifestyle templates

No competitor brand names, products, photography, claims or layouts were copied.
